What is Aerobics? How to Get Started (+ Best Types of Exercise for Beginners)

Aerobics refers to a system of physical conditioning that combines rhythmic aerobic exercise with stretching and strength training routines. The goal is to improve various elements of fitness, including flexibility, muscular strength, and cardiovascular fitness1. When you engage in aerobic exercise, your breathing controls the amount of oxygen that reaches your muscles, helping you burn energy and move2.
